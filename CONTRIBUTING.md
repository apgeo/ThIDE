# Contributing to TherionProc

Contributions are welcome — just open a pull request or push a branch. There is
**no CLA and no DCO sign-off**: you don't need to do anything beyond submitting
your code.

## Licensing of contributions

> By contributing, you agree your contributions are licensed under the project's
> licenses (MPL-2.0 for the libraries, GPL-3.0-or-later for the app).

In practice this is "inbound = outbound": a change under `src/**` is contributed
under **MPL-2.0**, and a change under `TherionProc/**` under
**GPL-3.0-or-later**. See [LICENSING.md](LICENSING.md) for the full picture.

Please only contribute code you have the right to contribute — your own work, or
work you are authorized to submit under these licenses.
